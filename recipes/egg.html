Soft-Boiled Egg Recipe

Description

I'm allergic to raw egg yolk but I eat this anyway because I love it.

Ingredients

  • Egg

Steps

  1. Heat water in a pot, with the water just high enough to completely cover the egg, until it comes to a boil.
  2. Use a slotted spoon to gently place the egg in the water.
  3. Cook for 6 minutes, and then remove.
  4. Peel away and nom!
